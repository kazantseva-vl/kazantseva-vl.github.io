Implementation of Schematic and PCB
using Altium Designer

Creation of the schematic, design footprints, and the printed circuit board (PCB)
based on given specifications using Altium Designer.

In this project I made:

Schematic of circuit

It's raphical representation of the electronic components and their interconnections

Components footprint

It's physical representation of the component on a printed circuit board (PCB)

Printed circuit board (PCB)

It is the actual implementation of the circuit design, where components are mounted

The goal of the Schematic

is to convey the circuit's logical and functional aspects.
It serves as a blueprint for further stages, such as PCB design or simulation.

Learn More

Printed Circuit Board application

making a PCB involves translating the circuit design from the schematic into a physical board layout.
It includes placing components, routing traces, setting design rules, and generating manufacturing files.

Result

The completed schematic and PCB design, along with all the necessary footprints, are ready for further stages of the circuit fabrication process.
This design can find application in various aspects of our daily lives, including consumer electronics, industrial automation, telecommunications, and medical devices.
The accurate representation of the circuit connections and the optimized PCB layout contribute to the reliability and functionality of the final product.
